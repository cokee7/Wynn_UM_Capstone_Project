Getting Started with FinSight

Welcome to FinSight, your intelligent financial insights platform. This guide will help you get up and running quickly with our platform.

Quick Start

  1. Create your account by registering with your email
  2. Login to access your personalized dashboard
  3. Explore the trending topics and insights
  4. Customize your dashboard according to your preferences

FinSight continuously scans financial news sources to provide you with the most relevant and timely information. Our advanced algorithms analyze sentiment and trends to help you make informed decisions.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble getting started, feel free to contact our support team at support@finsight.com

Understanding Your Dashboard

Your FinSight dashboard is the command center for all your financial insights. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Top Stats: Quick overview of trending topics, sentiment scores, and activity metrics
  • Trend Charts: Visual representation of key financial topics and their performance over time
  • Sentiment Analysis: Gauge market sentiment for specific sectors or companies
  • News Feed: Latest relevant news articles with sentiment indicators

The dashboard refreshes data automatically, but you can also manually refresh by clicking the "Refresh Data" button in the top right corner.

Generating and Using Reports

FinSight allows you to generate detailed reports for deeper analysis:

  1. Navigate to the "Generate Report" page from the main navigation
  2. Select the topics and time period you're interested in
  3. Choose the type of analysis you need (trend analysis, sentiment breakdown, etc.)
  4. Click "Generate" to create your report
  5. Download or share your report as needed

Pro Tip

Schedule recurring reports to be delivered to your email for consistent monitoring of key topics.

Customizing Your Experience

Make FinSight work for your specific needs by customizing these elements:

  • Dashboard Layout: Arrange widgets according to your preference
  • Focus Topics: Select specific financial topics or companies to track
  • Alert Settings: Define thresholds for notifications
  • Data Visualization: Choose your preferred chart types and color schemes

All customizations are automatically saved to your profile and will be applied each time you log in.

Managing Your FinSight Account

Keep your account details up-to-date and secure. Here's how to manage your profile and settings:

  • Updating Profile Information: Navigate to your "Profile" or "Settings" page (usually accessible from the top-right user menu). Here you can update your name, email address, and other personal details. Remember to save your changes.
  • Changing Your Password: For security reasons, it's good practice to change your password periodically. Go to the "Security" or "Change Password" section in your account settings. You'll typically need to enter your current password and then your new password twice.

Account Security Tips

  • Use a strong, unique password for your FinSight account.
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails asking for your login details.
  • Log out of your account when using shared computers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are answers to some common questions about using FinSight:

How often is the dashboard data updated?

The core data on your dashboard, such as trending topics and sentiment scores, is typically updated in near real-time or every few minutes to provide you with the most current insights. Some historical chart data might update less frequently (e.g., daily).

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your registered email address, and we will send you instructions on how to reset your password.

Can I export data or reports from FinSight?

Yes, the "Reports" section allows you to generate detailed analyses which can usually be downloaded in common formats like PDF for offline use or sharing.
